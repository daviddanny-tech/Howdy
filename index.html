real stories from our howdy community

  • Kamesh
  • traveler

    • "Kodaikanal was the perfect escape from the city heat! The cool breeze, misty mountains, and the serene lakes made it a refreshing break. Cycling around the lake was a fun activity, and the homemade chocolates were a treat!"

  • Kanika
  • traveler

    • "My visit to Meenakshi Temple in Madurai was a spiritual experience. The architecture is simply majestic, with its intricate carvings and vibrant colors. Watching the evening aarti was mesmerizing. It’s a must-see for anyone interested in culture and history."

  • Giri
  • traveler

    • "Ooty is absolutely beautiful! The lush green hills, tea plantations, and the toy train ride made it feel like a dream. We had a relaxing time boating at Ooty Lake and exploring the botanical gardens. I’d recommend visiting in off-season to avoid crowds."

  • Sujit kumar
  • traveler

    • "Pondicherry was a delightful blend of Indian and French culture. Strolling along the Promenade Beach during sunset was peaceful, and the French cafes served some of the best pastries I’ve ever had. The quaint colonial architecture added to the charm"

  • Krishnaveni Shree
  • traveler

    • "Rameswaram was a deeply spiritual trip for me. The journey across the Pamban Bridge, with the sea on both sides, was stunning. Taking a dip in the sacred waters and visiting the Ramanathaswamy Temple was an unforgettable experience. The island’s beauty is serene and untouched."

  • Tharun ramesh
  • traveler

    • "Thanjavur’s Brihadeeswarar Temple is a marvel of ancient architecture. The sheer size of the temple and the towering gopuram left me speechless. It's a place where history comes alive, and the craftsmanship from over a thousand years ago still stands strong."
